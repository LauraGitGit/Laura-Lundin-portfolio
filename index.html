––– About me

Once upon a time, I was deep in operating rooms, my world was filled with heartbeats, precision, and quiet bravery. After over 6.5 years assisting in complex surgeries and supporting patients through high-pressure situations, I decided to shift my focus to another form of problem-solving: coding.
I wanted to build, to design, to create.

In 2018, I discovered front-end development — and I couldn't stop.What began as curiosity quickly became an obsession.
