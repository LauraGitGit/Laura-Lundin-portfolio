––– About me

Once upon a time, I was deep in operating rooms, my world was filled with heartbeats, precision, and quiet bravery. After over six years assisting in complex surgeries and supporting patients through high-pressure situations, I decided to shift my focus to another form of problem-solving: coding.
I wanted to build, to design, to create.

Why Front-end?

In 2018, I discovered front-end development — and I couldn't stop.What began as curiosity quickly became an obsession.Coding felt like a new kind of challenge: one where I could build,test,debug, and improve things in real time. I loved the blend of logic and visual design.To me, front-end is like growing something fromnothing
— it's visual, responsive, and alive.
